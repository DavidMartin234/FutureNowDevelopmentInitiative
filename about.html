FUTURENOW DEVELOPMENT INITIATIVE

About us

FutureNow Development Initiative is a

non-governmental organization founded in Bauchi

State, Nigeria in the year 2021, With the aim

of creating vibrant platforms for human

development through enhancing

skills and capacities

Aims

Creating vibrant platforms for human development

Aim at promoting inclusiveness in planning, monitoring and evaluation of right and policies

Aim at increasing participation and development through capacity building trainings

to create equal opportunity for human development irrespective of his or her gender,religion, age or ethnic group

Aim at easing idleness through empowerment programs and skill aquisition trainings

Objectives

The group will fulfil the aim by:

1. Providing safe space for human development

2. Strenghtening the capacity of the communities to cater and improve children

wellbeing by increasing access to education, livelihood(adequate food) and healthcare

3. Community sensitization and mobilization on harmful practices,stigma, discrimination etc

through creative presentation such as drama, debates, radio engagement etc.

4. Increasing participation in civic engagement

5. Promoting and encauraging education to families in communities

6. Provision of basic needs i.e. food, clothing, medical care, etc to children with disabilities, etc in various communities in the district and neighboring districts.
